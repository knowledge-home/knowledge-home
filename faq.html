FAQ

Questions, answered clearly

Quick answers about donations, transparency, reporting, and partnerships.

Donations
Receipt

Do I get a receipt or confirmation?

Yes - we confirm by email. As payments get connected, official receipts and downloadable confirmations can be added.

Donations
Monthly

Can I donate monthly (auto donation)?

Yes - recurring donations can be supported. If it isn’t live yet, message us and we’ll help you set it up.

Donations
Bulk

Can I do a bulk donation (one-time large support)?

Yes. Bulk support can fund kits, devices, or program delivery. Share your amount, region preference, and timeline on the contact page.

Transparency
Tracking

How do you track where support goes?

We publish allocation summaries and progress updates on the Tracking page. Proof (receipts/invoices/notes) is shared where applicable.

Partnership
CSR Sponsor

Can a company sponsor or run a campaign?

Yes. We can align a sponsorship or campaign with kits, mentorship, or digital access and share a clear reporting timeline.

Volunteering
Remote

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes - mentorship, content, documentation, outreach, and data/reporting support can be remote-friendly.
