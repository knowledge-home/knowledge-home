Proof & verification

What we share, when available

We aim to share proof responsibly—balancing transparency with dignity and privacy.

  • Invoices or purchase summaries for kits and supplies
  • Partner confirmation notes and delivery lists
  • Mentorship schedules and session counts
  • Photos or brief notes when appropriate and consented
How this evolves

From summaries to live reporting

As systems mature, reports can move from static summaries to real-time updates connected directly to tracking and payments.

  • Payment-linked allocation updates
  • Automated monthly summaries
  • Clear status labels: completed, ongoing, planned
